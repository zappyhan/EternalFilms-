Welcome To Eternal Films!

Hello! welcome to our website managed by Desmond Wong and Zhang Han.

Who We Are

We are two passionate travelers who love capturing the world's beauty through photography and videography. My partner, Desmond, is skilled in taking breathtaking shots of landscapes, cities, and cultures, having traveled to countries like Germany, Thailand, and Japan. His work not only showcases stunning visuals but also tells compelling stories. I founded Eternal Films to help share his incredible work with a wider audience, allowing others to experience the world through his lens. Together, we aim to inspire and preserve the essence of our travels, one frame at a time.

What We Do

We are a team of passionate photographers, videographers, editors, and programmers who founded Eternal Films to showcase our work and offer professional visual services. As a non-profit initiative, we provide high-quality photoshoots and video-making services for friends and family, capturing meaningful moments and telling compelling stories. We believe everyone deserves beautifully captured memories and are dedicated to sharing our creativity with others.
